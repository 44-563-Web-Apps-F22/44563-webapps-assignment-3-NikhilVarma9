Palermo Parrot

Palermo Parrot is the mascot for the palermo Islanders. He had served as the mascot for the Palermo Dragons Arena Football team until that league's 2009 demise. What made him unique was the fact that he wore two sets of colors, depending on the team he rooted for. He wore royal blue and orange.
