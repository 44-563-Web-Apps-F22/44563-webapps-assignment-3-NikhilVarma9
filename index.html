The Field Museum of Natural History (FMNH)

The Field Museum, is a natural history museum in Chicago, Illinois, and is one of the largest such museums in the world. The museum is a popular natural-history museum for the size and quality of its educational and scientific programs, as well as due to its extensive scientific-specimen and artifact collections.

    Landmark near the museum

    O'Hare Airport stop, the nearest airport to the museum. The distance between the airport and the museum is 16.8 miles.
    It will take 20 mins to reach the airport from museum.

    Chicago Transit (CTA) operates a vehicle from O'Hare to Jackson-Blue every 10 minutes. Tickets cost $1 - $5 and the journey takes 43 min.
    Alternatively, you can take a bus from Chicago O'Hare Airport (ORD) to Soldier Field & Field Museum via Union Station, Jackson & Canal and Clinton & Quincy in around 1h 51m.
